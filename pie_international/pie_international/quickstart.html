PIE Quick Start Guide

Congratulations! You're only 3 steps away from using PIE!

Step 1: Get the scheme

What sounds do those diacritics stand for? The scheme is designed to be very easy to learn! Here are the PDF version and image version of the scheme. Save and print them for quick reference!

Step 2: Know how to zoom

Initially you may find diacritics too small to see, so you need to know:

  • To zoom the current site: Hold down the  Ctrl  key (or  ⌘  for Mac) and scroll your mouse wheel forward/backward to zoom in/out the current site. If you don't have a mouse, press  Ctrl  and  +  to zoom in,  Ctrl  and  -  to zoom out.
  • To zoom all sites: Go to the Chrome menu (  ) >  Settings  >  Show advanced settings...  >  Web content  >  Page zoom  and change this setting from  100%  to  125%  (or other zoom levels you like).
Step 3: Start using PIE!

Just click the icon on Chrome's toolbar so that it becomes , which means PIE is turned on. Another click will turn it off. Now browse the English Wikipedia to see PIE in action!

We also highly recommend another free Chrome extension Word Translator which shows meaning of English words and works perfectly with PIE!

You can also

  • Check out PIE's Options (right-click and then click "Options"). There you can choose between various modes such as Full, Lite and Extra Lite, specify your own fonts for rendering PIE text, specify which websites should be transformed, and more.
  • Bookmark our project's official website, which is your definitive source for everything PIE!
